ABOUT US

The story behind Gentleman's Garage — where heritage meets innovation.

Our Heritage

Founded by a collective of classic car restorers and AI engineers, Gentleman's Garage was born from a simple idea: what if the precision of artificial intelligence could enhance the timeless craft of automotive restoration?

Our workshop combines decades of hands-on craftsmanship with state-of-the-art AI diagnostics, predictive modeling, and design optimization. Every vehicle that enters our garage receives the kind of attention that only this unique fusion of old and new can provide.

Our Philosophy

We believe that classic cars are more than machines — they're works of art, pieces of history, and expressions of identity. Our AI-enhanced approach doesn't replace the human touch; it elevates it. From paint-matching algorithms that recreate factory-perfect finishes to structural analysis that ensures safety without compromising authenticity, every tool we use serves the car's legacy.

Wine & Wheels

Our passion extends beyond the garage. Through our exclusive vineyard tours and wine events, we bring together two worlds of refinement: fine automobiles and fine wines. It's a lifestyle, not just a service.
